You do not need an 80-page specification to start. You do need enough clarity that a senior engineer can challenge scope and propose a first slice.

## What to include

- Problem and desired business outcome.
- Primary users and their jobs.
- Happy-path workflow step by step.
- Must-have vs later.
- Integrations and data sources.
- Constraints: security, compliance, deadline, budget band.
- Definition of done for v1.

## What to skip early

- Exhaustive UI pixel specs before the workflow is proven.
- Edge cases for scenarios you have never seen in reality.
- Technology mandates unless you have a real constraint.

## How vendors use a good brief

A clear brief produces better estimates, fewer change orders, and faster first demos. A vague brief produces padded quotes.
