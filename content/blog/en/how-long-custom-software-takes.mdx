“How long does custom software take?” is the sibling of the cost question. Honest partners answer with phases, not one heroic date.

## Typical phase bands

- **Discovery + first thin slice:** often 2–6 weeks depending on clarity.
- **MVP usable by real users:** commonly 1–3 months for focused scope.
- **Hardening + integrations + multi-role expansion:** additional months as complexity grows.

## What makes timelines slip

- Unowned decisions on your side.
- Hidden integrations discovered late.
- Expanding “MVP” mid-flight.
- No staging or QA until the end.

## How to set a timeline you can trust

1. Define done for the first release in writing.
2. Schedule weekly demos.
3. Separate must-haves from later bets.
4. Re-forecast after sprint one with real evidence.

ConaiSoft plans delivery as sprint milestones with working software — so dates are based on progress, not optimism.
