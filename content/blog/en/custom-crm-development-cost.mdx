“How much does a custom CRM cost?” depends on whether you are cloning a generic CRM or encoding a unique revenue process.

## Buy/configure SaaS CRM when…

- Your pipeline stages are standard.
- Reporting needs are common.
- Integrations are mainstream.

## Build custom (or heavily extend) when…

- Your workflow is a competitive advantage.
- Off-the-shelf forces painful workarounds for pricing, inventory, or partner portals.
- You need embedded AI on proprietary data with strict permissions.

## Cost drivers

- Number of roles and permissions,
- integrations (billing, ERP, messaging),
- migration of historical data,
- reporting complexity,
- mobile/offline needs.
