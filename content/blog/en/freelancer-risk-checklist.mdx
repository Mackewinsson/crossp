Hiring freelancers is attractive: lower rate, quick start, less process. For a landing page, that can be fine. For a product that holds customer data, payments, or AI workflows, the risk profile changes.

## Red flags

- No automated tests and no CI pipeline.
- Code lives only on their laptop or a personal account.
- No written ownership of source code and credentials.
- Bus factor of one: if they disappear, nobody can continue.
- “It works on my machine” with no staging environment.

## Non-negotiables in the contract

- Repository under your organization from day one.
- Documented handover of domains, cloud, and API keys.
- Definition of done that includes tests for critical flows.
- Weekly demos with working software, not just status emails.
