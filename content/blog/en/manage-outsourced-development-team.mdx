Hiring an external team does not remove the need for product ownership. It changes how you exercise it.

## Weekly operating rhythm

- Shared backlog with ranked priorities.
- Demo of working software every week.
- Written decisions (what changed and why).
- Single product owner on your side with authority to decide.

## Acceptance, not status theater

Ask “What can a user do now that they could not last week?” Status slides without software are a smell.

## Communication rules

- Async updates in one channel of record.
- Escalation path for blockers within 24–48 hours.
- No critical secrets in personal chats.

## Metrics that matter

- Cycle time to production for small changes.
- Defects found after release on critical paths.
- Your team’s ability to run the system without the vendor present.
